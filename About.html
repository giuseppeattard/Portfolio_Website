About

From a very young age photography and arts have always been the admiration of Giuseppe and it was only until his first year of University that he started taking the latter seriously. During the first experience working with photography, art and graphic design during Diploma studies in 2015, a connection was juicily made and it led to the journey where this year he will be graduating from the University of Malta.

This course has presented him with the opportunity to continuously improve his skills especially when it comes to Graphic design and Photography. With professional experience when it comes to photography and videography in conjunction with the use of aerial cameras through work, throughout his free time as well as experience through student organisations, his skills are always put to the test and continuously improved.

The mastering of photography and Adobe Suite programs not only in theory but also in everyday circumstances gives him an edge on the job.
